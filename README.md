# 🌹 Mood Garden

Mood Garden is a mood tracker that I built to use inside my Notion dashboard.

Instead of just choosing how I'm feeling, I wanted something that felt calming and beautiful to open every day. I can choose my mood, write a quick journal entry, and it automatically saves everything to my Mood Journal database in Notion.

## Features

* 🌸 Choose from different moods
* ✍️ Write a journal entry
* 🌞 Morning, Noon, and Night check-ins
* 🎨 The colors of the widget change depending on the mood I choose
* 🌹 Rose bloom animation when the widget opens
* 📖 Automatically creates a new entry in my Notion Mood Journal
